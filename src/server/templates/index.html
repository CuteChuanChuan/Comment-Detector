PTT is the largest online forum in Taiwan, with an average of nearly 20,000 users online per hour.
Due to the rise of information warfare and the increase in cyber warriors manipulating public opinion, an unbiased platform is necessary for PTT users to help detect these malicious behaviors.
The PTT Comment Detector is that platform, providing objective and descriptive data for PTT users.

There are over 20,000 boards on PTT. However, due to time and cost considerations, two specific boards have been selected.
One is the Gossiping board, which is the most popular.
The other is the HatePolitics board, created specifically for PTT users to express their political opinions.

There are three parts in PTT Commenter Detector

  • Overview: Displays the latest keyword trends on PTT, as well as the top 10 trending and popular articles in the Gossiping board.
  • Keyword: Shows the most active commenters related to the keyword you entered and their connections with each other.
  • Commenter: Presents a graph illustrating the activity time and a word cloud for the commenter you specified.
